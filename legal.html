Official Sources

Use Official IRS Sources for Verification

The authoritative source for U.S. federal tax rules is the Internal Revenue Service. For specific figures, current brackets, deduction amounts, and withholding rules, refer directly to:

  • irs.gov — The IRS official website
  • IRS Publication 505 — Tax Withholding and Estimated Tax
  • IRS Publication 15 (Circular E) — Employer's Tax Guide
  • IRS Publication 17 — Your Federal Income Tax (general reference)

These publications are updated regularly and represent authoritative guidance. This reference site summarizes and explains concepts for informational purposes and is not a substitute for these official resources.
